Refund Policy

Welcome to Al Ehsan Media & Tech.

At Al-Ehsan Media & Tech, we prioritize delivering high-quality digital products and ensuring customer satisfaction. However, due to the nature of digital content, we maintain the following refund policy:

No Refunds on Digital Products

Once a digital product is purchased and delivered (downloaded or accessed), the transaction is considered complete, and refunds will not be issued under normal circumstances. This is because:

  • Digital files are delivered instantly after purchase
  • There is no way to "return" a digital product to prevent continued use
  • Our products include extensive previews to ensure informed purchases

Exceptions

Refunds or replacements may be considered only in the following cases:

  • The file is corrupted, incomplete, or cannot be opened
  • The file delivered is significantly different from the product description or preview images
  • There are technical issues preventing you from accessing the product, and our support team cannot resolve them
  • Duplicate purchase made in error (must be reported within 24 hours)

How to Request Support

If you experience any issues with your purchase:

  • Contact us: Reach out via email at support@alehsanmt.com with your order details and a description of the issue
  • Provide evidence: Include screenshots or other documentation of the problem
  • Resolution Process: Our support team will investigate and work to resolve your issue within 3-5 business days

Processing Refunds

If your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
  • The refund will be credited to your original method of payment
  • You will receive email confirmation once processed
