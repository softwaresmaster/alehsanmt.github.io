Do You Have Questions Appliance Repairs

01. What types of appliances do you repair?

We repair a wide range of home appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, microwave ovens, water purifiers, and more. Whether it's a small kitchen appliance or a large home unit, our technicians are trained to handle various brands and models.

02. How do I schedule a repair service?

You can schedule a repair service by contacting us via phone, WhatsApp, or through our website. Simply provide your appliance details, the issue you're facing, and your preferred time slot. We will confirm the appointment and send a technician to your location.

03. What are your service charges?

Service charges vary depending on the type of appliance and the nature of the repair. We provide a transparent pricing structure and will give you an estimate before any work begins. Additional charges may apply for spare parts if needed.

04. Do you offer any warranty on repairs?

Yes, we offer a warranty on our repair services. The duration of the warranty depends on the type of repair performed. Typically, it ranges from 30 days to 6 months. If the issue recurs within the warranty period, we will fix it at no additional cost.
